# Siyarix Trademark & Brand Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026  
**Version:** 1.0.0

This policy governs the use of the Siyarix name, logos, visual assets, and related brand identifiers. It is designed to protect the integrity and reputation of the project while remaining open-source friendly and supportive of community growth.

---

## 1. Protected Marks

The following are protected trademarks and brand assets of the Siyarix project:

| Mark / Asset | Type | Protection Scope |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Siyarix** | Word mark | The name in any typographic representation |
| **Siyarix Logo** | Design mark | The official logo artwork (see `assets/logo.png`) |
| **Siyarix wordmark** | Word mark | Stylized text representation of the name |
| **Siyarix icon** | Design mark | The standalone icon element of the logo |

All rights in these marks are reserved by the Siyarix project and its authorized representatives.

---

## 2. Permitted Use (No Permission Required)

You may use the Siyarix name and branding in the following ways without prior written approval, provided the use is accurate, non-confusing, and not disparaging:

| Use Case | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Accurate attribution** | "Built with Siyarix" or "Powered by Siyarix" |
| **Educational content** | Tutorials, articles, videos, blog posts, and books about Siyarix |
| **Community discussion** | Social media posts, forum discussions, conference talks, meetups |
| **Fair reference** | Referencing Siyarix in comparison with other tools or platforms |
| **Integration mentions** | "Integrates with Siyarix" for compatible plugins, tools, or services |
| **Fork attribution** | Clearly labeling a fork as "Unofficial fork of Siyarix" |
| **Academic citation** | Citing Siyarix in research papers, theses, or academic publications |

When using the name in these contexts, use the form "Siyarix" (capitalized) or "Siyarix project."

---

## 3. Use Requiring Permission

The following uses require **explicit written permission** from the project maintainers. Requests should be submitted via a GitHub issue or private communication:

| Use Case | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Using the Siyarix logo as part of your product, service, or company branding | Prevents confusion about endorsement |
| Including "Siyarix" in your product name (e.g., "Siyarix Enterprise," "Siyarix Manager") | Avoids misleading branding |
| Official partnership, certification, or compatibility badges | Must meet quality and compatibility standards |
| Merchandise (t-shirts, stickers, mugs) bearing Siyarix branding | Must meet quality standards |
| Domain names containing "Siyarix" | Prevents domain squatting and impersonation |
| Using Siyarix branding in commercial offerings based on Siyarix | Requires compliance review |

Permission requests will be evaluated based on the proposed use, alignment with project values, and potential for confusion or reputational impact.

---

## 4. Prohibited Use

The following uses are **not permitted** under any circumstances:

| Prohibited Activity | Example |
|---------------------|---------|
| **Impersonation** | Distributing modified versions under the name "Siyarix" without clear and prominent differentiation |
| **Misleading branding** | Using Siyarix branding to imply official endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation of unrelated products or services |
| **Fake official distributions** | Distributing modified code under the unmodified Siyarix name without indicating modifications |
| **Domain squatting** | Registering domains like `siyarix-security.com`, `siyarix.cloud`, etc. without permission |
| **Trademark dilution** | Using the mark in a way that harms the reputation, distinctiveness, or goodwill of the project |
| **Malicious use** | Forking the project and using the Siyarix name for malware, scams, phishing, disinformation, or illegal activities |
| **False endorsement** | Implying that Siyarix endorses, recommends, or is affiliated with your product or service without permission |

---

## 5. Community Forks

We support the open-source ecosystem and encourage innovation through forking. If you fork Siyarix:

- **Do** rename your fork to a distinct name that does not include "Siyarix" as the primary identifier
- **Do** clearly state in your project's README that it is a fork of the original Siyarix project
- **Do** include the required AGPL-3.0-or-later license notices
- **Do not** use the official Siyarix logo without clear modification or differentiation
- **Do not** imply your fork is endorsed, maintained, or supported by the original Siyarix project

### Recommended Fork Attribution

```markdown
This project is a fork of [Siyarix](https://github.com/mufthakherul/siyarix).
It is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Siyarix project.
```

## 7. Logo & Visual Assets

- The official logo and visual assets are available in the project repository under `assets/`
- Do not modify the logo in ways that could cause confusion about the nature of your project
- Do not use the logo in a manner that suggests endorsement without explicit permission
- The logo should always be used in its original form without distortions, color changes, or additional effects unless modified for your specific fork (which must be clearly marked as unofficial)

---

## 8. Reporting Violations

If you suspect trademark or brand misuse:

1. **Open a GitHub issue** in the main repository tagged with `trademark` label
2. **For urgent cases** (active impersonation, phishing, malware distribution), contact the maintainers via private channels

The maintainer team will review reports and take appropriate action, which may include:

- Contacting the violator with a cease-and-desist request
- Reporting to domain registrars, hosting providers, or platform operators
- Public notice of unauthorized or misleading use
- Legal action in cases of willful infringement, fraud, or consumer harm

---

## 9. Enforcement

Violation of this policy may result in:

- A formal request to cease the infringing activity
- Public notice clarifying the unauthorized nature of the use
- Reporting to relevant platforms, registrars, or marketplaces
- Legal action in cases of willful infringement, trademark dilution, fraud, or consumer harm
- Referral to law enforcement for cases involving impersonation, fraud, or illegal activity

---

*This policy is designed to protect the Siyarix brand while fostering a healthy, open ecosystem. It is not intended to restrict legitimate discussion, education, research, or innovation involving the project.*
